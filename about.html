About DriveEasy

DriveEasy is a modern car rental platform designed specifically for students and young professionals. We understand the need for affordable, reliable transportation for college trips, weekend getaways, and intercity travel.

Founded as a B.Tech mini project, DriveEasy aims to revolutionize the car rental industry by making it more accessible and student-friendly.

Our mission is to make car rentals simple, secure, and affordable, empowering students to explore freely without breaking the bank.
